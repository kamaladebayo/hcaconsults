Our Impact

Healthcare Analytics (HCA) Consults is a healthcare marketing and communications organization, with core competence in creating avenues to foster productive conversations, provide networking opportunities with key public and private stakeholders/ policy makers in various sectors through our virtual & physical conferences, networking and exhibitions.

HCA Consults is concerned with introducing brands, organizations and products focused on achieving effective healthcare delivery in Africa regardless of the sector. With an efficient experience in the state of the health sector in developing countries, HCA consults has hosted multiple effective health promotion events through Lagos Health Summit (LHS), UK-Nigeria Health Tech Fest and Nigeria Health Investment Forum (NHIF) in a bid to take action to minimize healthcare delivery inequality, strengthen the healthcare system and influence the potentials of intersectoral collaborations.

HCA Consults helps promotes its partners and sponsors brand goal(s) by creating a business enabling environment, fostering productive conversations with key intersectoral stakeholders and providing networking opportunities for various participants/attendees.

At Healthcare Analytics Consults, we believe in a healthier, more vibrant Africa—a continent where every individual has access to quality healthcare, where innovation transforms lives, and where collaboration fuels progress.

Africa's healthcare landscape faces challenges, but within these challenges, we see immense potential. Our journey began with a simple yet profound question: "What if we could reimagine healthcare, not as it is, but as it could be?"

We are on a mission to rejuvenate Africa's healthcare system, one event at a time. We envision a future where health promotion isn't just a concept but a way of life—a future where healthcare transcends borders, connecting communities, experts, and innovators.

We measure our success not just by the number of events we host, but by the lives we touch and the partnerships we forge. We've witnessed innovations that save lives, policies that empower communities, and collaborations that transcend boundaries.

We are not just hosting events; we are fostering change, nurturing dreams, and breathing life into a vision—a vision where healthcare knows no bounds.

Welcome to a new era of healthcare. Welcome to HCA Consults.

OUR EVENTS

Lagos Health Summit

Welcome to the heart of healthcare innovation in Africa—welcome to Lagos Health Summit. This annual gathering is not just an event; it's a catalyst for change, a platform where ideas flourish, and a testament to our commitment to creating a healthier Nigeria and by extension, a healthier Africa.

At Lagos Health Summit, we envision a Nigeria where healthcare knows no limits. Our vision is to transform healthcare into a right, not a privilege, and to empower individuals, communities, and organizations to take charge of their health and wellness.

We believe that the most effective solutions to healthcare challenges emerge when diverse minds come together. Our summit is a melting pot of thought leaders, policymakers, healthcare practitioners, and innovators, all united by a common purpose—to inspire change.

Over the years, Lagos Health Summit has been a catalyst for numerous healthcare initiatives, policies, and collaborations. We've witnessed innovations that change lives, discussions that shape policies, and partnerships that transcend borders.

Nigeria Healthcare Investment Forum

THE NIGERIA HEALTHCARE INVESTMENT FORUM stands as a unique conference and networking platform. It will connect financial institutions, developmental partners with business leaders, and key decision-makers in the health sector, catalyzing conversations on funding and investment opportunities in the health sector. This event paves the way for exploring innovative financing models, addressing gaps in healthcare financing, and adopting both immediate and long-term growth, with profound impacts on the improving the delivery and equitable access to quality healthcare services.

Nigeria Healthcare Investment Forum is the gateway to exploring funding opportunities and attracting the right investment into the Nigerian health sector. Attendees will immerse in a diverse array of sector-focused discussions, enriched by enlightening keynote and panel sessions, insightful presentations, and valuable interactions with industry, business development and investment experts.

The UK-Nigeria Health-Tech Fest

THE UK-NIGERIA HEALTH-TECH FEST is a unique conference and networking platform that connects industry stakeholders, financial institutions, developmental partners, policymakers, and key decision-makers in the health sector to create conversations on funding and investment opportunities in the sector.

The forum will create a platform that will foster innovative financing models, bridge healthcare financial gaps, and promote both short-term and long-term growth of the sector to have a great impact on the teeming populace. Attendees at the event will gain from a huge variety of sector-promoting discourse, keynote and panel sessions, presentations, industry stakeholders, and board room interactions, as the event seeks to advance funding and investment opportunities in the health sector.
