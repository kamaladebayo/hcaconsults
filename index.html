OUR STORY

Healthcare Analytics (HCA) consult was established in 2020 by a group of healthcare advocates cut across different professionals in the health sector and other sectors in a bid to take actions to minimize healthcare delivery inequality and transform it into quality for all regardless of race, religion, and socio-economic status by fostering productive conversations and providing networking opportunities with key public and private stakeholders/ policy makers in the health sector while creating a business enabling environment through events, exhibitions, digital marketing, interactive deliberations, and speaking presentations.

HCA consult is a healthcare marketing and communications organization, whose aim is to help introduce brands, organizations and products focused on achieving effective healthcare delivery in Africa. HCA consult is concerned with creating a means to deliberate the health sectors in Africa and a point of reference for Intersectorial collaborations.

VISION: To create an avenue to deliberate and collaborate on improving the health sector to achieve quality healthcare for all regardless of socio-economic status.

MISSION: A place where key health stakeholders can collaborate with various sectoral stakeholders.

ABOUT LAGOS HEALTH SUMMIT 3.0

Lagos Health Summit is a two-day strategic conference and exhibition slated to happen once annually. Two summits down with many more leading positive and paramount deliberations by creating a platform for debate and developing actionable strategies that can help shape the future of healthcare in Nigeria and Africa with more than 10 organizations in attendance for the maiden edition of the summit and over 20 organizations present for the second edition of the summit.
