ABOUT

THE NIGERIA HEALTHCARE INVESTMENT FORUM is a unique conference and networking platform that connects industry stakeholders, financial institutions, developmental partners, policymakers, and key decision-makers in the health sector to create conversations on funding and investment opportunities in the sector.

The forum will create a platform that will foster innovative financing models, bridge healthcare financial gaps, and promote both short-term and long-term growth of the sector to have a great impact on the teeming populace. Attendees at the event will gain from a huge variety of sector-promoting discourse, keynote and panel sessions, presentations, industry stakeholders, and board room interactions, as the event seeks to advance funding and investment opportunities in the health sector.

This event is organized by HCA Consults, a leading healthcare marketing firm that is advancing effective healthcare delivery in Africa through fostering productive conversations and offering brand promotion opportunities.
